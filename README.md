The project on LinkedIn product dissection involves a detailed analysis of the LinkedIn platform, exploring its features, functionalities, and overall design.
The objective is to understand how LinkedIn addresses real-world challenges in professional networking, job searching, and skill development.
The dissection will cover aspects such as the user interface, user experience, algorithms, data privacy, and the impact of LinkedIn's features on solving practical problems in the professional landscape.
By dissecting LinkedIn's product, the project aims to provide valuable insights into the platform's innovations and solutions for users' needs in the evolving world of work.
